Take full control of your Watts Vision+ smart heating system — directly from Homey.

No more switching between apps. Set temperatures, switch modes, start a boost and automate your entire heating schedule from one place.

---

WHAT YOU CAN DO

Control every thermostat zone from Homey:
- See live room temperatures across all zones
- Set target temperatures instantly from the device tile
- Switch between Auto/Schedule, Comfort, Reduced/ECO, Boost and Anti-freeze modes
- Start a boost heat session for 30 minutes up to 24 hours
- Know exactly when your boiler is actively firing with the Actively Heating indicator

Get notified when something needs attention:
- Push notification when a device reports an error (e.g. battery failure)
- Built-in diagnostic log to help troubleshoot — copy to clipboard in one tap

Automate your heating with Flows:
- "When I leave home → Set all thermostats to Anti-freeze"
- "When temperature drops below 17°C → Start boost for 1 hour"
- "When boost ends → Send a notification"
- "When we arrive home → Set all thermostats to Auto/Schedule"

---

FLOW CARDS

7 actions including Set ALL thermostats to ECO/Anti-freeze/Schedule in one card.
3 conditions: Is heating, Boost active, Device has error.
8 triggers: Mode changed, Temperature thresholds, Boost started/ended, Error occurred/cleared.

---

REQUIREMENTS

- Homey Pro (SDK 3, Homey v5.0.0 or newer)
- A Watts Vision+ account (smarthome.wattselectronics.com)
- A Watts Vision+ gateway connected to the internet
- At least one thermostat zone configured in the Watts Vision+ app

---

SETUP

1. Add device → Watts Vision+
2. Sign in with your Watts Vision+ email and password
3. All thermostat zones are discovered automatically
4. Select the zones you want and you're done

---

TROUBLESHOOTING

If login fails, verify your credentials at smarthome.wattselectronics.com first.
For diagnostics, tap Configure on the app page to view the built-in log.
